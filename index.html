About Me

I'm a third-year undergraduate student at Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT), Bhopal, pursuing electronics and communication engineering, with a strong interest in game development, graphics programming, and embedded engineering.

My past work experiences include being a Research Intern at the Visualization and Graphics Lab, CSA department of the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, where I worked on interactive visualisations of topological data using contour trees and made a contour tree visualizer tool based on WebGL2. AudioPrism - Audio Stem Segmentation Model

AudioPrism interface and demo.

AudioPrism is an UNet based audio stem segmentation model for separating audio segments in the browser. Stems are downloadable and can be used to do mixing, editing or analysis of the audio.

  • Waveform overlay on player that spotlights the audio's nature.
  • Support for 16 different stems, that makes the separatation pin pointed.
  • Runs fully on the web so collaborators can use the software without installs.
