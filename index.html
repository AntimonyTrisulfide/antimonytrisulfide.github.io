About Me

I'm a final-year B.Tech student in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT), Bhopal. I like building graphics tools, browser apps, games, and embedded systems projects.

I worked as a Research Intern at the Visualization and Graphics Lab, CSA department of the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru. There, I built a WebGL2 contour tree visualizer for selecting and inspecting topological data.

I also led game development for BISKit, a Bureau of Indian Standards learning platform, and have been building AudioPrism, PReSPIDAR, and small interactive simulations.

Current Focus

I am currently focused on Data Structures & Algorithms, C++ game development, shaders, OpenGL/WebGL, and applied machine learning. I am looking for work where I can build graphics, embedded systems, scientific tools, or applied software.
