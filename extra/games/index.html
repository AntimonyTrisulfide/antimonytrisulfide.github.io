Classic Games Collection

Welcome to my collection of classic games! These are browser-based implementations of timeless arcade games, built with modern web technologies.

🐍 Snake Game (YAOSG)

Yet Another Online Snake Game - A modern take on the classic Snake arcade game. Guide your snake to collect food while avoiding walls and your own tail!

  • Controls: Arrow keys to move
  • Goal: Collect fruit to grow your snake and increase your score
  • Challenge: Don't hit the walls or your own body!
  • Features: Responsive design, retro pixel art graphics
